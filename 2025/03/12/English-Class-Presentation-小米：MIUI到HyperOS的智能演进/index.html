[English Class Presentation] 小米：MIUI到HyperOS的智能演进

槿鸢2025-03-122025-03-12

从手机系统起家的小米

Xiaomi’s Rise with Mobile Systems

  • 背景 (Background): 小米由雷军创立，最初是一家互联网公司，专注于为安卓用户提供更好用的系统替代方案。当时安卓原生系统功能简陋，小米抓住了机会，推出了深度定制的 MIUI。
    Founded by Lei Jun, Xiaomi started as an internet company aiming to offer a better system alternative for Android users. Back then, stock Android was basic, and Xiaomi seized the chance to launch the deeply customized MIUI.
  • 起点 (Starting Point): 2010年，小米以 MIUI 系统起步，开启了智能设备之路。
    In 2010, Xiaomi began with the MIUI system, launching its journey in smart devices.
  • 核心战略 (Core Strategy): 以操作系统为核心，连接手机与智能硬件，构建 IoT 生态。
    With operating systems at its core, Xiaomi connects phones and smart hardware to build an IoT ecosystem.
  • 演进动力 (Driving Evolution): 从 MIUI 的开放创新到 HyperOS 的全生态互联，小米不断推动系统升级。
    From MIUI’s open innovation to HyperOS’s full ecosystem connectivity, Xiaomi keeps advancing its systems.

MIUI 的黄金时代：开放与自由

The Golden Age of MIUI: Openness and Freedom

  • MIUI 的诞生 (Birth of MIUI): 2010年，小米推出了基于 Android 深度定制的 MIUI 系统。
    In 2010, Xiaomi launched MIUI, a deeply customized system based on Android.

  • MIUI 的特点 (Features of MIUI):

    • 美观易用 (Beautiful & User-Friendly): 界面简洁，操作流畅，深度本地化适配。
      A clean interface, smooth operation, and deep local adaptations.
    • 高度可定制 (Highly Customizable): 支持主题、图标、字体自由更换，满足个性化需求。
      Customizable themes, icons, and fonts to meet personal needs.
    • 刷机文化 (Flashing Culture): 鼓励用户刷机，构建了庞大的开发者社区。
      Encouraged flashing, fostering a large developer community.
    • 每周更新 (Weekly Updates): 快速迭代，积极响应用户反馈。
      Weekly updates with fast responses to user feedback.

  • MIUI 每个版本的演进 (The Evolution of Each MIUI Version):

    • MIUI V1 (2010年8月16日): “为发烧而生”
      “Born for Enthusiasts”
      特点：由3个工程师打造，专注短信、联系人、电话，奠定互联网开发模式和“橙色星期五”传统，首批100位“梦想赞助商”参与。
      Features: Built by 3 engineers, focused on SMS, contacts, and calls, establishing an internet development model and “Orange Friday” tradition with 100 initial “dream sponsors.”
      *
    • MIUI V2 (2010年10月29日): “功能初现”
      “Features Emerge”
      特点：优化系统应用（音乐、网盘、桌面等），首创拨号界面 T9 搜索、DOCK 栏支持文件夹，奠定原创功能基础。
      Features: Optimized system apps (music, cloud, desktop), introduced T9 dialer search and folder-supporting DOCK bar, setting the stage for original features.
    • MIUI V3 (2011年3月25日): “个性开启”
      “Personalization Begins”
      特点：推出百变锁屏与主题（MAML 语言与 MORE 引擎），首创访客模式、音量键唤醒屏幕，个性化体验起飞。
      Features: Introduced versatile lock screens and themes (MAML language and MORE engine), pioneered guest mode and volume key screen wake, kicking off personalization.
    • MIUI V4 (2012年1月19日): “服务扩展”
      “Service Expansion”
      特点：上线云服务、应用商店等，首创钢琴音拨号、闹钟智能跳过节假日、陌生来电首声静音，用户破千万。
      Features: Launched cloud services and app store, introduced piano dial tones, smart alarm skipping holidays, and silent first ring for unknown calls; users exceeded 10 million.
    • MIUI V5 (2013年3月1日): “设计统一”
      “Unified Design”
      特点：T 型结构统一设计语言，首创夜景防抖、运行时权限（领先安卓三年）、二维码分享 WiFi，用户隐私与功能并重。
      Features: Unified design with T-shaped structure, pioneered night mode stabilization, runtime permissions (3 years ahead of Android), and QR WiFi sharing, balancing privacy and features.
    • MIUI 6 (2014年8月16日): “极简美学”
      “Minimalist Aesthetics”
      特点：全新极简设计，首创标记号码拦截、不重要通知、流量校正，用户突破 1 亿。
      Features: New minimalist design, introduced number marking blocking, unimportant notification filtering, and data correction; users surpassed 100 million.
    • MIUI 7 (2015年8月13日): “拒绝单调，为你设计”
      “Say No to Monotony, Designed for You”
      特点：推出多套主题与巨无霸字体，首创宝宝相册、一键换机、小米快传，个性化与实用兼得。
      Features: Introduced multiple themes and giant fonts, pioneered baby album, one-tap device switch, and Mi Drop, blending personalization with utility.
    • MIUI 8 (2016年5月10日): “就是好用”
      “Simply Easy to Use”
      特点：万花筒灵感设计，小米兰亭字体首发，加入分屏、应用双开、倒班助手，实用性大增。
      Features: Kaleidoscope-inspired design, debuted Mi Lanting font, added split-screen, app cloning, and shift assistant, boosting usability.
    • MIUI 9 (2017年7月26日): “快如闪电”
      “Fast as Lightning”
      特点：聚焦流畅性，首创全面屏手势、小爱同学、信息助手，用户突破 3 亿。
      Features: Focused on smoothness, introduced full-screen gestures, XiaoAI assistant, and info assistant; users exceeded 300 million.
    • MIUI 10 (2018年5月31日): “AI加持的全面屏系统”
      “AI-Powered Full-Screen System”
      特点：优化全面屏体验，AI 预加载与自然音效，小爱深度整合，提升操作效率。
      Features: Enhanced full-screen experience, AI preloading and natural sound system, deep XiaoAI integration, improving efficiency.
    • MIUI 11 (2019年9月24日): “效率革新，声色双全”
      “Efficiency Revolution, Vivid Sound and Vision”
      特点：动态字体与声音体系，万象息屏，Mi Work/Go 套件，首创 AI 通话与地震预警。
      Features: Dynamic fonts and sound system, Always-On Display, Mi Work/Go suites, introduced AI calls and earthquake alerts.
    • MIUI 12/12.5 (2020年4月27日): “触碰想象，感受真实” 触碰想象，感受卡顿
      “Touch Imagination, Feel Reality”
      特点：米柚光锥动效、超级壁纸，全局自由窗口，隐私保护三大件（照明弹等）。
      Features: Mi Light Cone animations, Super Wallpapers, global free windows, and three privacy tools (Flare, etc.).
    • MIUI 13 (2021年12月28日): “开始·连接万物” 开始，万物卡顿
      “Start Connecting Everything”
      特点：流畅度提升最高 52%，全链路反诈，小米妙享中心开启跨端互联。
      Features: Smoothness improved by up to 52%, full-link anti-fraud, Mi Magic Center enabling cross-device connectivity.
    • MIUI 14 (2022年12月11日): “锐意新生” 结果精简了也不行
      “Boldly Reborn”
      特点：精简系统，优化资源占用，强化 IoT 互联，为 HyperOS 铺路。
      Features: Streamlined system, optimized resource use, enhanced IoT connectivity, paving the way for HyperOS.

  • 意义 (Significance): MIUI 赋予用户更多控制权，塑造了小米“为发烧而生”的品牌形象。
    MIUI gave users more control and built Xiaomi’s “born for enthusiasts” image.

MIUI 后期的问题与转变

Issues and Shifts in Later MIUI

  • 背景 (Background): 随着小米业务扩展到智能硬件和 IoT 生态，MIUI 的定位从“为发烧友服务”转向支持更广泛的商业战略，导致开放性减少并暴露出维护问题。
    As Xiaomi expanded into smart hardware and IoT ecosystems, MIUI’s focus shifted from “serving enthusiasts” to supporting a broader commercial strategy, reducing openness and exposing maintenance issues.
  • 表现 (Changes):
    • 提高解锁门槛 (Higher Bootloader Unlock Threshold): 小米开始提高解锁 Bootloader 的门槛，用户需申请权限并等待数天才能刷机，以提升安全性。
      Xiaomi raised the threshold for unlocking the Bootloader, requiring users to apply for permission and wait days to flash, enhancing security.
    • 减少自定义 (Less Customization): 后期版本对 Root 权限和系统修改的管控更严格，用户自由度下降。
      Later versions tightened control over Root access and system modifications, reducing user freedom.
    • 生态导向 (Ecosystem Focus): MIUI 更注重与小米自家硬件和服务整合，不再像早期那样兼容广泛的第三方设备。
      MIUI shifted to integrate with Xiaomi’s own hardware and services, less compatible with third-party devices.
    • 系统维护缓慢 (Slow System Updates): 更新频率从每周一次变为数月一次，老机型常被忽视，系统优化跟不上硬件老化。
      Update frequency dropped from weekly to every few months, with older models often neglected and system optimization lagging behind hardware aging.
    • 设备 EOL 快 (Fast End of Life): 小米设备支持周期较短，许多中低端机型两三年内停止更新，用户被迫升级硬件。
      Xiaomi devices have short support cycles, with many mid-to-low-end models ceasing updates within two to three years, forcing users to upgrade hardware.
  • 意义 (Significance): 这些问题反映了小米从极客文化向商业化生态的转型，为 HyperOS 的全生态战略铺路，但系统维护和设备支持的短板在 HyperOS 2 中仍未完全解决。
    These issues reflect Xiaomi’s shift from geek culture to a commercial ecosystem, paving the way for HyperOS’s full-ecosystem strategy, but shortcomings in system maintenance and device support persist in HyperOS 2.

HyperOS 的转型：从 MIUI 到全生态

HyperOS Transformation: From MIUI to a Full Ecosystem


  • HyperOS 的发布 (HyperOS Launch): 2023年，小米推出全新操作系统 HyperOS，标志着 MIUI 时代的结束，小米澎湃OS 正式登场。
    In 2023, Xiaomi unveiled HyperOS, marking the end of the MIUI era and the rise of Xiaomi’s HyperOS.

    • 传承与接力 (Legacy and Transition): “万物基于 MIUI”的接力棒传到了 HyperOS 手中。
      The baton of “everything based on MIUI” has been passed to HyperOS.

  • 转型原因 (Reasons for Transformation):

    • 全生态愿景 (Full Ecosystem Vision): 以人为中心，打造“人车家全生态”操作系统。
      Centered on people, building an OS for the “human-car-home full ecosystem.”
    • 万物互联 (Internet of Everything): 开启全生态、万物互联的系统时代。
      Ushering in an era of a fully connected, smart ecosystem.
    • 跨端协同 (Cross-Device Collaboration): 打通手机、平板、手表、汽车等设备。
      Connecting phones, tablets, watches, cars, and more.
    • 安全与稳定 (Security & Stability): 提升系统安全性和稳定性。
      Enhancing system security and stability.

  • 特点 (Features):

    • 深度优化内核 (Optimized Kernel): 基于 Android 和 Linux 深度定制，提升性能和资源管理。
      A deeply customized kernel based on Android and Linux, boosting performance and resource management.
    • 澎湃智联 (HyperConnect): 设备无缝互联，提供流畅体验。
      Seamless device connectivity for a smooth experience.
    • 以人为本 (Human-Centric): 注重用户需求，优化全场景交互。
      User-focused, optimizing interactions across all scenarios.
    • 更封闭生态 (Closed Ecosystem): 限制刷机，强化官方生态体验。
      A more closed ecosystem, emphasizing official integration.

  • HyperOS 2 的亮点 (Highlights of HyperOS 2): 2024年10月，小米发布 HyperOS 2，基于 Android 15，进一步强化 AI 和互联体验。
    In October 2024, Xiaomi released HyperOS 2, based on Android 15, further enhancing AI and connectivity.

    • HyperCore 内核优化 (HyperCore Optimization): 小米自研微架构调度器，降低 CPU 空闲时间，提升任务效率。
      Xiaomi’s in-house microarchitecture scheduler reduces CPU idle time and boosts task efficiency.
    • HyperConnect 升级 (HyperConnect Upgrade): 支持跨设备双摄流传输，与苹果设备部分兼容（中国大陆）。
      Supports dual-camera streaming across devices and partial compatibility with Apple devices (Mainland China).
    • HyperAI 智能功能 (HyperAI Features): 小爱同学升级为“超级小爱”，提供 AI 驱动的锁屏动态效果、防诈识别、实时字幕等功能。2025年3月，超级小爱接入 DeepSeek，进一步增强代码生成、文章写作和复杂推理能力。
      XiaoAI upgrades to “Super XiaoAI,” offering AI-driven lock screen effects, anti-fraud detection, real-time subtitles, and more. In March 2025, Super XiaoAI integrates DeepSeek, enhancing code generation, article writing, and complex reasoning.
    • 视觉与交互升级 (Visual & Interaction Upgrades): AI 壁纸根据用户喜好生成个性化背景，全新锁屏自定义支持多样组件，动效升级更流畅自然，新增 3D 天气系统，应用启动延迟减少最高 54.9%。
      AI wallpapers generate personalized backgrounds based on user preferences, new lock screen customization supports diverse widgets, upgraded animations feel smoother and natural, plus a 3D weather system and app launch delays reduced by up to 54.9%.

  • 不断收紧的权限
    小米在发布HyperOS之后，就开始收紧Bootloader权限了，就是因为那些臭开挂的，发格机脚本的，还有故意弄虚焊去骗保的。到了OS2之后，直接一刀砍死，社区账号要5级，还要去社区申请解锁权限，答题（小米高考），为了降低售后成本，强化官方生态体验，也只能这么做，因为社区太多事多的人了）玩机社区也没以前那么热闹了。。。





Vela：物联网软件平台

Vela: Xiaomi’s IoT Software Platform

  • 定位 (Positioning): 小米基于开源实时操作系统 NuttX 打造的物联网嵌入式软件平台。
    An IoT embedded software platform built by Xiaomi based on the open-source real-time OS NuttX.
  • 特点 (Features):
    • 统一服务 (Unified Services): 为多种物联网硬件提供一致的软件支持。
      Offers consistent software support across various IoT hardware.
    • 丰富组件 (Rich Components): 提供多样化的组件和易用框架。
      Provides diverse components and an easy-to-use framework.
    • 低功耗与实时性 (Low Power & Real-Time): 优化功耗，确保快速响应。
      Optimized for low power and real-time performance.
    • 生态协同 (Ecosystem Integration): 与 HyperOS 配合，打通碎片化的 IoT 场景。
      Works with HyperOS to unify fragmented IoT applications.
  • 应用场景 (Applications): 用于智能手环、手表及其他物联网设备。
    Used in smart bands, watches, and other IoT devices.

总结与展望

Conclusion and Outlook

  • 演进 (Evolution): 从 MIUI 到 HyperOS，小米展现了技术升级。
    From MIUI to HyperOS, Xiaomi shows tech progress.
  • 未来 (Future): 深耕“手机 x AIoT”，目标成为全球智能科技领导者。
    Focusing on “phones x AIoT” to lead in smart tech globally.
